SculptCMS

2016.01.23 22:49




SculptCMS is a free, flat-file CMS de signed for straight-forward usage. There is no special markup, and no extra steps. Simply drop your markdown files into the content/pages directory to begin. The page title is the file title. Sculpt is compatible with Markdown Extra.

SculptCMS was written during a vacation, and is the finalizing of the custom CMS I use on side projects.
